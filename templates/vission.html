Vission

Save life india vision is: To pave way for a safer and better tommorrow. Safer, by bringing blood donors and those in need to a common platform. Better, by providing every person what he/she deserves the most, best education. Our aim in next 5 years, To be the real hope of every Indian in search of a voluntary blood donor. To set up a well organised infrastructure through out the country to cater to the education of the under resourced by way of maintaining a repository of contributed books and providing as many resources as possible for rural child education.

MISSION :

To make the best use of contemporary technologies in delivering a promising web portal to bring together all the blood donors in India; thereby fulfilling every blood request in the country. To provide a common platform for those who have a zeal to support education of the under resourced yet meritorious students.
